AuraSync - Refund Policy
========================

AuraSync offers refunds only on a case-by-case basis.

1. Eligibility
- Refund requests must be made by emailing support@aurasync.info
- Include your payment receipt or license code.

2. Limitations
- Refunds are not guaranteed.
- We typically do not provide refunds after significant use of premium features.

3. Processing
- If approved, refunds will be issued back to your original payment method.
