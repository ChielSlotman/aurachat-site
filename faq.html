Frequently Asked Questions

What does the free tier include?

One floating image with your own upload. Works on any chat page.

What does Premium include?

Unlimited avatars plus extra visual and audio effects and priority image loading.

How do I activate Premium?

After payment you land on the success page and get a one‑time activation code. Paste it in the extension under “Activate Premium”.

What if I get a new computer or browser?

Ask for a fresh activation code. Your remaining time stays the same.

How do I cancel?

Open the billing portal from the Pricing page. Subscriptions are managed by Stripe.
