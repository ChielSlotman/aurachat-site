AuraSync - Privacy Policy
=========================

AuraSync respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect
- Email address: only when you redeem a premium code or contact support.
- License codes and tokens: stored securely to validate premium access.
- Extension settings (avatars, effects, preferences): stored locally in your browser.

2. What We Do Not Collect
- We do not track your browsing activity.
- We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.
- We do not use cookies (see Cookie Policy).

3. Third-Party Services
- Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Stripe may collect data necessary to complete transactions.

4. Your Rights
- You may request access to or deletion of your stored data by contacting us at support@aurasync.info.

5. Security
- We use encrypted storage and secure APIs to protect license tokens and account data.

6. Contact
- Questions? Email support@aurasync.info
