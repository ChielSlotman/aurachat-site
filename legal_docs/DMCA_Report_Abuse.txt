AuraSync - DMCA / Report Abuse
==============================

If you need to report copyright infringement or abuse, please follow the steps below.

1. How to Report
- Email your report to support@aurasync.info
- Include your name, contact details, the URL or screenshot of the infringing material, and a statement of good faith.

2. Our Response
- We will review valid DMCA requests and take appropriate action.
- Abuse reports (such as harmful content or misuse of AuraSync) will also be reviewed.

3. Contact
- support@aurasync.info
